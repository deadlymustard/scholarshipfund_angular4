About Kevin

On March 12, 2011, Kevin Thomas Gilbert passed away, when his car veered off the road while he was on his way to an early morning baseball practice. Kevin passed away from severe brain trauma.

Kev was a senior at Hunterdon Central Regional High School (HCRHS), an honor roll student and athlete. He was incredibly passionate about baseball. He had been a starter for the HCRHS varsity baseball team since his sophomore year and had a dream of becoming a Major League Baseball player. Kevin had planned to attend Temple University on an academic and athletic scholarship.

Kevin constantly displayed an unselfish passion for life and a kindness towards others, both on and off the field, and was an inspiration to many. The stories of his kindness are inspirational.

As an organ donor, Kevin continued to help people even after the loss of his life, saving five individuals with successful transplants, and changing countless of other lives through bone and tissue donation.

He was, and will forever be, Our Angel in the Outfield.

About the Scholarship Fund

The Kevin Gilbert Scholarship Fund is a non-profit organization whose goal is to help college-bound students continue to make a difference, to live their life with unselfish passion, and to achieve their dreams. The Kevin Gilbert Scholarship Fund sponsors an annual scholarship that is available to any graduating Hunterdon Central Regional High School (HCRHS) senior who has a passion and a dream, and has had a positive effect on others through kindness and respect.
