Registration

Registration for the 2020 Kevin Gilbert Wiffle Ball Tournament is now open. Register your team here. Registration is $100 per team. Registration includes a free t-shirt and a two-game guarantee.

Venue Information

Alcohol is permitted at the pavilion only. BYOB. There are a limited number of grills available located in the pavilion area for use on first come first serve basis. Please bring your own cooking supplies and ingredients, as they will not be provided.

Purpose

This tournament was inspired by Kev and his love for all things baseball. Wiffle ball was a common past time activity in the backyard of the Gilbert home, so we decided to bring those great memories back in the form of a fundraiser. All proceeds from this tournament will go to the Kevin Gilbert Scholarship Foundation. Click here to read more about Kevin and the Scholarship Fund.
