1st Annual Wiffle Ball Tournament (2017)

Twelve teams and numerous volunteers and spectators came together on Sunday, August 27th at Pickell Park in Whitehouse Station in loving memory of Kev. A big thank you to all who came out to play/volunteer/watch!

The competitive league championship game was one for the history books! Godzilla Farmers and Big Boppers battled for over an hour and a half for bragging rights to the first ever winner of the tournament. The Godzilla Farmers came out on top in a nail-biting 2-1 finish.

Top two teams in the finals- the Godzilla Farmers (black) and the Big Boppers (blue). From left to right: TJ Mordeci, Justin Cvetkovski, Ryan Del Hoyo, Chad Hunt, Bryan Noonan, Jake Maffucci, Richie Masini, and Chris Suseck

Friendly league champions, the Mastodons, beat the Goofballs 13-1 in the finals.

Mastodons! From left to right: Matt Prall, Nick Andreychak, Chris Smith, Scott Niedhammer and Bill Pier

In addition to the tournament, friends and family of Kevin competed in the Home Run Derby. TJ Mordeci won the derby with 5 homeruns!
